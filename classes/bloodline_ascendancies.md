# Bloodline Ascendancies — Reference

A separate ascendancy system, obtained by defeating specific bosses. **Replaces the 4th regular ascendancy notable** — you get 3 class notables + 1 Bloodline notable instead of 4 class notables.

**Verified:** [verified 2026-03] via poewiki.net

To acquire: defeat the relevant boss, then interact with the altar it spawns to take or change your Bloodline Ascendancy class.

---

## All 8 Bloodline Ascendancies

### Aul Bloodline
**Source:** Aul, the Crystal King (Delve — Crystal King's Throne, depth 130+)
**Theme:** Charge-conditional aura buffs, defensive bonuses for empty armour sockets

Notables:
- **Legacy of the King** — Wrath/Discipline 50% increased aura effect at max/min Power Charges
- **The King's Heritage** — Determination/Anger 50% increased effect at min/max Endurance Charges
- **The King's Might** — Grace/Hatred 50% increased effect at min/max Frenzy Charges
- **Precursor's Release** — No stun if helmet has no socketed gems; no extra crit damage if gloves have no socketed gems; cannot be slowed below base if boots have no socketed gems; elemental ailment immunity if body armour has no socketed gems

*Build note: Only useful if running those specific auras (Wrath, Discipline, Anger, Determination, Grace, Hatred). Precursor's Release requires empty armour slots — incompatible with any linked skill setup.*

### Farrul Bloodline
**Source:** Farrul, First of the Plains (Bestiary — Farrul's Den)
**Theme:** Minions, warcry speed, spectral tigers on crit

Notables:
- **Huntleader** — Trigger Level 20 Summon Spectral Tiger on Critical Strike
- **Primal Roar** — 4% increased attack speed per minion (max 80%); minions deal 80% increased damage after warcrying
- **Farrul's Will / Fertile Will / Wild Will** — Raised Beast Spectres get 3 additional modifiers + Farrul's Presence aura

*Build note: Huntleader requires crits (Precise Technique prevents crits). Primal Roar requires minions. Irrelevant.*

### Catarina Bloodline
**Source:** Catarina, Master of Undeath (Mastermind's Lair)
**Theme:** Minions, offerings

Notables:
- **Death Offering** — Offerings kill affected targets when they expire; 50% increased offering effect
- **Umbral Army** — Scaling bonuses per minion type (attack damage per zombie, spell damage per skeleton, defences per spectre, etc.)
- **Prolonged Servitude** — Minions can't die but expire 6 sec after reaching 1 life; 20% more damage on low life

*Build note: No minions, no offerings. Irrelevant.*

---

### Chaos Bloodline
**Source:** The Trialmaster (Ultimatum encounters)
**Theme:** Vaal skills, corrupted item bonuses

Minor nodes: 8% increased max Life; Gain 1 Vaal Soul per second

Notables:
- **Corruption's Embrace** — You have "Shepherd of Souls" at 4+ corrupted items; "Everlasting Sacrifice" at 6+; "Sacrifice of Blood" at 8+
  - ⚠ *Buff effects of Shepherd of Souls, Everlasting Sacrifice, Sacrifice of Blood not documented on wiki — verify in-game*
- **Deceitful Servant (Priest of Yaomac)** — Vaal skills 30% less souls, 20% reduced prevention duration, 20% chance to regain souls on use
- **Deceitful Servant (Priest of Kopec)** — Vaal skills 40% more souls, +1 stored use, 1% more damage per soul required
- **Deceitful Servant (Priest of Kamasa)** — Vaal skills 50% longer duration, 50% more aura effect, 50% more AoE

*Build note: Minor nodes (8% life, 1 Vaal soul/sec) are nice. Corruption's Embrace is interesting if the build has 4-8 corrupted items (check current state). Buff effects unknown — needs in-game verification.*

### Lycia Bloodline
**Source:** Lycia, Herald of the Scourge (Forbidden Sanctum — Beyond encounter)
**Theme:** Chaos damage, heralds, profane ground

Notables:
- **Bitter Heresy** — Creates Profane Ground (instead of Consecrated); inflicts Malediction on enemies; grants Chaotic Might to allies on profane ground
- **Sinner Saint** — 67% of Fire/Cold/Lightning Damage Converted to Chaos Damage
- **Farewell to Flesh** — Herald skills 2% more buff effect per 1% mana reserved; heralds deal 1% more damage per 1% life reserved

*Build note: Sinner Saint converts elemental to chaos — blocked by Brutality for Shield Crush. Herald-focused doesn't apply. Irrelevant.*

### Nameless Bloodline
**Source:** The King in the Mists (Crux of Nothingness — Unlight Altar)
**Theme:** Extra ring slot at heavy cost

Minor nodes (each disables a slot and grants immunity):
- **Serpent's Price** — Can't use Amulets; Unaffected by Curses
- **Toad's Price** — Utility Flasks Disabled; Unaffected by Poison
- **Beetle's Price** — Can't use Belts; Unaffected by Bleeding

Notables:
- **The Unseen Hand** — +1 Ring Slot (requires all three minor nodes)
- **Unlight Silhouette** — Skills gain life cost equal to mana cost; adds chaos damage equal to 25% of life cost

*Build note: The Unseen Hand (3 rings) is powerful in isolation, but requires no amulet (lose Carnage Heart — leech, attributes, damage while leeching), no belt (lose life, chaos res, regen, anoint), and no utility flasks (lose shock immunity, fire res flask, movement). Far too much to give up.*

### Olroth Bloodline
**Source:** Olroth, Origin of the Fall (Expedition Logbook — Runic Stronghold)
**Theme:** Ward

Notables:
- **Enhanced Starlight** — 70% increased Ward; hits below 15% of Ward don't break Ward
- **Volatile Runes** — Trigger Level 20 Ward Shatter when Ward breaks
- **Runic Boon (River/Mountain/Sun)** — 5% chance to restore Ward on hit / 50% less DoT while Ward unbroken / 30% faster Ward restoration per hit taken

*Build note: No ward. Irrelevant.*

### Oshabi Bloodline
**Source:** Oshabi, Avatar of the Grove (Sacred Grove — Harvest)
**Theme:** Rage cycling, Elusive, Unleash

Minor nodes: +2 Rage on Attack Hit; 20% increased Elusive Effect; 20% increased Unleash Seal gain frequency

Notables:
- **The Wild Bear** — +20 to Maximum Rage (cap becomes 70); Lose all Rage on reaching Maximum Rage and gain Wild Savagery for 1 sec per 10 Rage lost
  - ⚠ *Wild Savagery buff effects not documented on wiki — verify in-game*
  - At 70 Rage cap: 7 seconds of Wild Savagery; rebuild time ~1.75 sec at 5 Rage/hit × 3 hits × 2.7 APS ≈ 78% uptime on buff
- **The Primal Owl** — Unleash +3 max seals; 5% more damage per Unleash reoccurrence
- **The Vivid Cat** — 50% increased Mark effect while Elusive; 50% chance to remove Elusive at 100% effect

*Build note: Minor Rage node (+2/hit) stacks with Rage Support (+3/hit) = 5 Rage/hit. Wild Bear's Rage cycling could be strong IF Wild Savagery is a good buff — unknown. Primal Owl and Vivid Cat irrelevant.*

## Summary for Physical Melee Attack Builds

Bloodline Ascendancy replaces the **4th class notable**. For MiragMaraBatato (Chieftain: Ngamahu + Tasalio + Valako + Sione), Sione is the candidate to drop — redundant in combat since kill-based power generation covers it, and its only real contribution is out-of-combat movement speed.

**Recommended: Oshabi Wild Bear** [calculated 2026-03, in-game verified]
- Wild Savagery: 100% increased phys, 10% ATS, cannot be stunned, ignore enemy PDR
- At 50 rage cap (30 base + 20 Wild Bear): 5s Wild Savagery per cycle
- **WS and rage ramp are concurrent** — rage immediately starts accumulating after trigger. WS is a buff, not a phase.
- Measured rage gain in combat: ~8/sec. Rage reaches 40 by WS expiry, then 1.25s more to cap.
- Cycle: 5s WS + 1.25s post-WS ramp = **6.25s total. 80% WS uptime.**
- DPS during WS (avg 20 rage): `126k × 1.322 × 1.177 = ~196k`
- DPS during post-WS ramp (avg 45 rage): `126k × 1.726 = ~217k`
- **Time-averaged: ~200k vs old sustained 30 rage ~187k = +7% tooltip DPS**
- PDR ignore on bosses (not in tooltip) is additional upside
- Sione comparison: Sione contributes near-zero combat DPS → clear win for Wild Bear
- Numbers assume: 91k hideout, 148k WS@0rage, 217k@45rage, gem base 0.8s, combat buffs ×1.16 frenzy more + 51% inc ATS

**Definitively not useful:** Aul, Farrul, Catarina, Lycia, Olroth, Nameless.
**Unknown buff effects (needs in-game check):** Chaos Corruption's Embrace (Shepherd of Souls / Everlasting Sacrifice).
