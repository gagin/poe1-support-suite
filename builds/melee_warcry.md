# Build Type: Melee Attack + Warcry

General mechanics that apply to any melee build using warcries.

---

## Multistrike and Warcry Exerts

**Intimidating Cry does NOT exert skills supported by Multistrike.**

Multistrike causes the skill to multi-hit (original + 2 repeats per use). Because of this, exerts from Intimidating Cry do not apply to the skill. Do not assume exert damage bonus is part of the DPS.

**Intimidating Cry is used for its other effects** (movement speed, Intimidate debuff, warcry power stacking with Sione).

If you want actual exerted attacks, use **Enduring Cry** (1 exert) or **Ancestral Cry** (multiple exerts), paired with skills that do NOT use Multistrike.

---

## Warcry Types

**Enduring Cry:** Generates endurance charges. Exerts 1 attack (irrelevant with Multistrike). With Urgent Orders: instant cast.

**Intimidating Cry:** Does not exert with Multistrike skills. Provides Intimidate debuff and mobility. With Sione (infinite warcry power): max buff immediately on use.

**Ancestral Cry:** Multiple exerts — only relevant if not using Multistrike.
