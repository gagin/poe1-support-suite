# Skill: Shield Crush + Impale

## Shield Crush Scaling

- Damage scales with the **armour/evasion value of the equipped shield** — flat damage added per 15 armour or evasion on the shield. A high-defence shield is core to the skill's damage floor.
- Main skill slot is the body armour 6-link.
- **"Damage with One Handed Weapons" does NOT apply to Shield Crush** — the skill scales from the shield's armour/evasion value, not the weapon. Avoid this mod on jewels/gear when optimising for Shield Crush DPS.

### Dawnbreaker Interactions
- "18% of cold / 12% of lightning / 20% of physical damage taken as fire" — this is **incoming** damage conversion (defensive), not outgoing. Does not conflict with Brutality.
- Scorch on block: applies fire exposure to enemies on block — lowers their fire resistance.

### Prismatic Eclipse — Socket Colors
- Red socket: 25% increased global physical damage each
- Green socket: 12% increased attack speed each
- Blue socket: 0.4% attack damage leeched as mana each

All-red = 75% increased physical (max damage). If already heavily stacked on increased damage, green sockets (attack speed = multiplicative scaling) may outperform.

## Precise Technique Keystone

"40% more attack damage when Accuracy Rating > Maximum Life. Cannot deal Critical Strikes."

- Requires maintaining accuracy > max life permanently
- All crit nodes and crit multiplier on gear are wasted — avoid them
- Stack accuracy on tree (Eagle Eye, Accuracy Mastery +500) and gear

## Impale — Full Setup Requirements

1. High impale chance (60%+ — from Impale Support or passives)
2. High impale effect (Savage Skewering, Forceful Skewering, Brutal Skewering notables)
3. **Impale Mastery: "Impale damage ignores enemy physical damage reduction"** — essential for endgame
4. Impale Support must be leveled — level 1 provides chance but near-zero impale effect, nearly useless

Impale lasts N hits per stack. More hits per stack = more efficient uptime.

### Deliberate Brutality Anoint — Does NOT Work Here
"Impales you inflict gain 50% increased Effect once 1 second of Duration has expired."
Requires the impale stack to survive on the enemy for 1 second before the bonus applies. With Multistrike, each attack use generates 3 hits. At 4.35 attacks/sec that is ~13 hits/sec. A 7-hit impale stack is consumed in 7 ÷ 13 ≈ 0.54 seconds — the threshold is never reached. This anoint is designed for slow heavy-hit builds, not Multistrike.
