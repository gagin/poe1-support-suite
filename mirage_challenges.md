## Remaining — Path to Gloves (need 8 more, reach 20)

### #10 Atlas Additions — *Very Easy*
5 of 11 league mechanics, 30× each (100× for Harvest and Ritual).
Best picks: Smuggler's Caches (click-and-forget), Legion, Ritual, Delirium (walk through mirror), Blight (one per map). Mirages duplicate mechanics so any Mirages in these maps count double.

### #11 Master Missives — *Easy*
2 of 4 master missions in T11+ maps, 30× each. Spec into Einhar + one other on Atlas tree. Einhar = capture all beasts in map. Alva = complete all incursions in map.

### #12 Sacred Sigils — *Easy (New Content)*
50 wishes each with Navira (Blue Jug), Kelari (White Dagger), Ruzhan (Red Sword). All three sigils appear at all stages — just vary your wish selection when you see each icon. Happens passively while doing other challenges.

### #14 Crafting Craze — *Very Easy*
15 of 19 currency types used specified amounts. Includes Transmutation, Alteration, Augmentation, Alchemy, Scouring, Fusing, Jeweller's, Chromatic, Regal, Chaos, Exalted, Blessed, etc. Skip Annulment (3×), Divine, Sacred if not already used. You've probably completed most of this already without knowing.

### #15 Maniacal Monsters — *Easy*
200 map bosses in rare maps with 80%+ IQ. This is just normal mapping. Destructive Play atlas passive helps.

### #17 Empowered Encounters — *Normal (New Content)*
4 of 11 league mechanics 15× each (50× for Harvest/Ultimatum) *inside Mirages*. Easiest: Ritual (25×, 3–4 per mirage), Legion (15×), Smuggler's Caches (15×). Spec Atlas toward your chosen mechanic and use Mirages actively.

### #18 Achieve Ascension — *Easy*
Use ascendancy device in all 4 labs + defeat one Bloodline boss. Already have 4 ascendancy points so labs are done — just need the Bloodline boss kill. Bloodline bosses: The Trialmaster (Ultimatum Scarab of Dueling), The King in the Mists (Audience With The King fragment), Catarina (Syndicate Medallion), Farrul (Menagerie beast recipe), or others from #28 list.

### #20 Peddler's Produce II — *Easy*
Four vendor recipes:
- **Unique item**: vendor 3× same non-corrupted unique → fresh copy
- **Orb of Fusing**: vendor any 6-link item
- **Regal Orb**: vendor full rare set (ilvl 75+, IDed or unIDed) or 3× same-named rares at 20% quality
- **Influenced item**: easiest — vendor normal-rarity influenced boots + Augmentation Orb + Quicksilver Flask

## Remaining — Path to Body Armour (challenges 21–28, need all 8)

### #16 Deadly Deeds — *Normal*
5 of 11 endgame encounters at area level 81+. Best options:
- Abyssal Depths ×5 — run Abyss in T14+ maps
- Domains of Timeless Conflict ×3 (25+ rewards) — buy emblems for a 4/5-way; not hard at all
- Blighted/Blight Ravaged Maps ×5 — use ring anoints (Silver+Opalescent for freeze, Violet+Indigo for Burning Ground)
- Simulacrum Waves ×60 — just waves, not completion; ~6 waves/attempt minimum to not waste money
- Atlas Runners ×30 — Kingsmarch passive income; rare maps only

### #19 Potent Premonitions — *Normal*
3 of 4 card types via Faustus (trade for specific div cards):
- Currency ×20 — easy, lots of cheap currency cards
- Gem ×10 — gem cards are cheap
- Two-Implicit Unique ×3 — skip this; specific cards only
- Six-Linked Item ×2 — can buy a 6-link div card set (The Celestial Justicar, The Demon etc.)

### #21 Lethal Leaders — *Normal*
6 of 8 bosses at level 80+. Easiest: Izaro (Eternal Lab with Goddess offering), K'tash/Ghorr/Beidat (Beyond bosses in maps), Medved/Vorana/Uhtred (Expedition Logbooks), Abyssal Lich (T14+ Abyss Depths), Omnitect (buy Chronicle of Atzoatl). Skip Heist (Vox Twins) and Kingsmarch RNG bosses.

### #22 Coveted Currency — *Normal (New Content)*
7 of 11 Mirage currencies used specified times. Best picks:
- Coin of Restoration ×5 — cheap, generic Mirage drop
- Coin of Desecration ×5 — cheap, generic drop
- Essence of Desolation ×3 — cheap, from Essence in Mirages
- Crystallised Rancour craft ×3 — from Harvest in Mirages
- Honoured/Sacred/Miraged Incubator ×10 — from Legion in Mirages
- Any Sinistral/Dextral Catalyst ×20 — from Ultimatum in Mirages
- Skip Volatile Vaal Orb (expensive, Beyond-specific drop)

### #23 Remarkable Realms
Complete all 17 unique maps. Most can be bought cheaply. **Doryani's Machinarium** is the hard one — Delve only from Ahuatotli boss at depth 50+, or group-buy. The Vinktar Square: vendor Agnerod North+East+South+West. Unique maps need to be visible on your Atlas to open.

### #24 Succinct Scarabs — *Normal*
8 of 26 scarab types used in T14+ rare maps, 30× each. With 4 scarab slots you get 5 progress per map → ~48 maps total if not wasting slots. Just pick your 8 most commonly used scarab types and use them consistently.

### #26 Cross Contamination — *Normal*
4 of 6 combined mechanic encounters. Easiest:
- Defeat Stygian Spire while affected by Shrine (Abyss Scarab of Edifice + Atlas shrines)
- Defeat Breach Marshal while Delirious (Atlas both, or scarabs)
- Defeat Blight Boss while affected by Tempest (Scarab of Radiant Storms + Blightspawn atlas)
- Skip: Unique Map Boss Possessed in Ritual, Delirium Boss in Sacred Grove (luck-heavy)

### #28 Seized Strength — *Normal*
7 of 11 bosses for Bloodline classes. Easiest:
- Trialmaster (Ultimatum Scarab of Dueling)
- Catarina (Syndicate Medallion fragment)
- Farrul (Menagerie — Farric Tiger Alpha)
- K'tash/Ghorr/Beidat or Tul/Esh (Breach)
- Olroth (Expedition Logbook with Knights of the Sun)
- Skip: Simulacrum (15 waves), Lycia (Sanctum)

Astrolabe-dependent. 100 Maven-witnessed bosses OR 200 Eldritch Altars in Astrolabe maps.

### #35 Instigative Invitations — *Normal*
All 8 invitations with 4+ modifiers. Just roll invitations until you get baby mods (no quantity req, just 4 mods). Straightforward with a functional build.

### #36 Nightmare Nemeses — *SKIP CANDIDATE*
4 of 5 Nightmare (ex-T17) maps with 175%+ IQ. Scarab of Stability helps. Still plays like T17.

### #37 Weeping Warlords Warfare — *Unknown (New Content)*

### #38 Tremendous Tempests — *SKIP CANDIDATE*
75 T16 rare Astrolabe maps with 150%+ IQ. Astrolabes at ~1 per 200 maps = currency dump.

### #39 Uber Undertaking — *SKIP CANDIDATE*
5 of 10 Uber pinnacle bosses at area level 85. Buy carries or skip.

### #40 Gallant Grinding Goals — *Normal*
4 of 6 heavy tasks. Best picks:
- Divine Font ×50 (8 Eternal Lab runs with Gift to the Goddess)
- Rare 4-mod monsters in T16 ×250 (passive while mapping with Beyond/Essence/Breach/Nemesis)

- T16 maps with scarabs ×1000 (skip unless deep into endgame)
- Skip: Level 100, Mirage T16 bosses ×100
