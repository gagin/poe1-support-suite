# Levelling tips

The biggest trick in getting more XP is not dying and losing XP.

## Core Strategy: Beyond + Breach

The most efficient XP method combines Beyond and Breach mechanics:

- **Beyond** (via Scarab of Invasion): Creates portals on rare/unique monster death, adding extra monsters that merge and compound with Breach spawns
- **Breach**: High monster density, easy to kill, good XP per pack

**Scarab setup:**
- Scarab of Invasion (Beyond)
- 2x Pack size scarabs (more magic monsters = more XP)
- Scarab of Multitudes (more abyss monsters) or Scarab of Teaching (shrine XP)

## Atlas Tree Nodes

Priority nodes:
- Beyond (extra monsters, merging)
- Domination (more shrines)
- Abyss (60% more XP from abyssal creatures)

## Domination Shrines

Shrines give massive XP bonuses. Stack multiple before doing Abysses:
- Scarab of Teachings: +30% XP per shrine buff
- Up to ~270% bonus with 9 shrines active
- Scout shrines first, then clear Abysses to cash in

## Magic Monsters

Magic monsters grant more XP than normal. Maximize with:
- Scarab of Dextral (magic monsters are suffix on map)
- Map mods with "+X% magic monsters"
- Pack size modifiers

## Map Strategy

- **Tier:** T16 maps — best XP/danger balance
- **Layouts:** Mesa, Dunes, Jungle Valley
- **Skip bosses** — slow, lower XP/hour
- **Full clear** better than speed-running
- **Avoid backtracking** — hurts XP efficiency

## Death Prevention

- **Omens of Amelioration** — Reduces death penalty from 10% to ~1.5%
- Use on each map when grinding XP

## Alternative: Breachstone Rotations

Join Chayula breachstone or 5-way Legion rotations. Each player contributes one map, runs 6x XP at 1x cost.
