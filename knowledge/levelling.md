# Levelling tips

The biggest trick in getting more XP is not dying and losing XP.

## Increased XP atlas tree nodes

There are several atlas nodes that give increased experience for certain mechanics — Abyss, Harvest, Ultimatum. Abyss is probably the simplest of the three.

## Breach

People report using Breach as more efficient than Abyss due to many monsters available and them not being hard. Be careful with the boss spawn though.

## Using white maps

Having harder maps does not convert to more experience directly. What one can do — run magic maps with "more magic monsters" and scarabs that boost magic monsters. Magic ones give more XP, and there are many of them in packs.

## Using scarab of dextral for magic monster maps

To further increase number of magic monsters, apply Scarab of Dextral to them — magic monsters are a suffix on the map. Rare monsters and two unique bosses are prefixes, by the way.
