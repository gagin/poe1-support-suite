# Path of Exile Base Valuation & Filterblade Adjustments

Filterblade categorizes top bases as Class A across different defensive categories and highlights them equally. In reality, pure Armour and pure Energy Shield bases have a massive advantage in the economy.

Legacy Brand & Attribute Value: Bases that are no longer the absolute highest tier can still be more valuable due to lower attribute requirements and legacy "brand recognition." For example, Eternal Burgonet is more expensive than Giantslayer Helmet despite technically being weaker, because 138 Str is much easier to manage than 153 Str.

Unique Chancing Value: Bases like the Hubris Circlet hold massive premium value because popular unique helmets are based on them from the era when they were the top base.

Actionable Rule: Filterblade should always be manually adjusted to account for these factors, demoting hybrid/evasion bases and prioritizing pure Armour/ES, accessible attribute thresholds, and popular unique bases.
